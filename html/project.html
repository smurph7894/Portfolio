      My Contributions

      Detail which parts of the project are yours. Which code did you design, write. test? How did you contribute to planning and running the team, which parts of the documentation did you write and help with. How did you lift as you climbed and demonstrate leadership.


      Project Experience

      What did you learn during this experience? What does what you experienced mean to you? How does the meaning that you have derived from these experiences fit into your academic pursuits, your career and life goals, as well as your community and society as a whole? What strengths did you learn from this experience? What were the humbling moments and how did you break through? What are the next steps for the project? What remains from your original vision for further development?
